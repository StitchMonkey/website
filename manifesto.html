Stitch it.

Within the dark depths of the “degen-verse”, lies a society of the chosen few who have come together to hastily construct beings that are soon to be released into the actual metaverse. Their unsettling features are sometimes mistaken as menacing, displeasing, and unpleasant. However, to the creators of these beings, they represent the future of our world, and their contributions will greatly affect the current state of the metaverse, whether people accept it or not… these degens are here to stay!
