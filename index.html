roadmap

Stage 1      ⬇

  • NFT creation: Only 5,555 first generation PFP NFTs will exist on the Solana blockchain.
  • Community building: Our community will be our greatest asset, not the NFTs. We want everyone in our community to grow and be successful.
  • Mint: This will enable holders to either sell, stake or hold their NFTs and holders can use them in our DegenLab. Holders will also automatically participate in royalty rev share from all marketplace sales.

Stage 2      ⬇

  • DegenLab Creation (Metaverse): The DegenLab is a 3D environment that will enable holders to "stitch up" portions of their NFT to build another new NFT. The portions they "tear out" can be sold, staked, or held. This will allow multiple avenues for revenue (for example, staking will generate tokens which can be used for the lab environment or sold). Creating a newer NFT will burn the previous generation which will increases the scarcity of the original collection.
  • DegenLab Reveal: The official launch of the lab.
  • DegenDAO: A community backed organization that will empower holders to vote on pivotal points of the project's future.

Stage 3      ⬇

  • Airdrops: Various types of assets and "building" tools will be dropped to holders to help with further customization and building of their new generation PFP NFTs.
  • Merch: Customized clothing that will be uniquely branded and "stitched" together, must like the existing NFTs created in the DegenLab. The merch will be exclusive to our most dedicated holders only.
  • Community Events (TBD): A slew of IRL events and meetups with fellow holders; other special meetups in the works. Some may be even be created by the DegenDAO.

Stage 4      ⬇

  • Merch drop: Dropping of the special merch to our most dedicated holders.
  • More details coming soon: We have so many more ideas that we're keeping a secret for now... we will reveal them so check periodically or follow our Twitter and join our Discord for all the details.
