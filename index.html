THE LAB (aka the “Degenverse”)

Inside a dark lab, using only the finest tools crafted from the metaverse and powered by the Solana blockchain, only the brave will be allowed to carve out their own creations (limited to 5,555). After obtaining the first generation of Stitch Monkeys, holders will have access to a secret lab, where they will be able to combine portions of their collections in order to re-construct a second generation of Stitch Monkeys, completely and wholly unique to their own liking. No two second-gen PFPs will be the same.
